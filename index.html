MoonFace

the

Mage

MoonFace the Mage is the bardic alias of South Wales based solo-artist Rhod Welsh. It's been 20 years since the inception of the MoonFace moniker; starting with solo shows in local bars, then releasing the first DIY EP: 'Folked up Beyond all Recognition' in 2005 to a welcoming audience.

MoonFace then continued to ride the music-scene-carousel with a UK tour and festival appearances; sharing the stage with the likes of Devendra Banhart, Seth Lakeman and Hayseed Dixie.

Through 2008-2010, the solo artist transformed into a 4-piece band under the guise of 'MoonFace and the Familiar Shapes? Proverbially melting the faces off a growing fan-base in the South Wales music scene.

And now, after 10 years in a silent slumber, MoonFace the Mage emerges like a mythical bard returning from a holy quest with a compendium of tales of godless things and mountain kings which meander across an acoustic soundscape in the shape of a Welsh bloke and an old guitar.
